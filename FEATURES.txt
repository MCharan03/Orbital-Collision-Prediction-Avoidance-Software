--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

   - Supports 10 satellite constellation groups:
       • Space Stations
       • Active Satellites
       • Starlink
       • GPS Operational
       • Galileo
       • Weather
       • Science
       • Earth Resources
       • Last 30 Days launches
       • Geostationary
   - Satellite emoji sprites (🛰️) with risk-based coloring
   - Hover tooltips showing: Name, NORAD ID, Altitude, Risk Level, Score, Velocity
   - Velocity direction arrow visible on hover/selection
   - Satellite search by name or NORAD ID
   - Scrollable satellite list with glassmorphic cards
   - Per-satellite risk badges (LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH)
   - Animated selection ring (expanding pulse) on selected satellite
   - Glow sphere for medium/high risk satellites
   - Pulsing animation for high-risk satellite markers
   - Orbit trail visualization when a satellite is selected (90-minute path)
   - Orbit trail glow layer for enhanced visibility


3. COLLISION DETECTION & PREDICTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   - Pairwise distance computation in TEME (True Equator Mean Equinox) frame
   - Relative velocity magnitude calculation between satellite pairs
   - Current-time collision detection at configurable threshold (default: 500 km)
   - Predictive closest approach tracking over 24-hour window
   - Time-of-Closest-Approach (TCA) computation
   - Multi-step time propagation with configurable step size
   - Co-location filter for ISS modules (ISS, Zarya, Zvezda, Unity, etc.)
   - Co-location filter for CSS modules (Tianhe, Wentian, Mengtian, etc.)
   - Collision events sorted by risk score (highest first)
   - Collision summary: total events, high/medium/low risk counts

   - Physics-based risk scoring (SGP4 propagation model)

   - Risk classification: LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH
   - Confidence score for AI predictions (0-100%)
   - Model disagreement detection (when Physics and AI disagree)
   - Explainability — "Why AI Predicted This" with reason list
   - Combined final risk level from both models
   - Per-satellite risk mapping from collision events
